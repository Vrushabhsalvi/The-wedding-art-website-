About Us

At The Wedding Art, we don’t just take photos—we tell stories. Founded in Mumbai by Shubham Chandiwade, our team specializes in capturing love, emotions, and the essence of every celebration. With experience in over 300+ weddings including destination shoots worldwide, we transform moments into cinematic memories. Our work blends elegance, creativity, and storytelling to make your wedding truly unforgettable.

Captured Moments That Tell Your Story

Every wedding we shoot becomes more than photos—it becomes a cinematic love story. From romantic pre-wedding moments to candid dance highlights and heartfelt vows, our Instagram-inspired approach focuses on capturing the real emotions and personal stories of each couple. Whether it’s joyful laughter, intimate looks, or unplanned moments of connection, we craft every frame with artistic intention and cinematic style.

A Creative Collaborative Team

Behind the lens is a creative team working seamlessly together to bring out the best in your celebration. Through behind-the-scenes creativity, natural storytelling, and attention to detail, we make every photoshoot an experience you’ll treasure. Our Instagram posts, reels, and stories reflect the passion and teamwork that goes into each project.

Global Experience With Local Touch

Based in Mumbai but traveling worldwide, we’ve captured weddings across cities and cultures, blending traditional moments with modern cinematic techniques. Each reel and gallery image reflects our dedication to preserving memories in their most authentic and beautiful form.
